Give me a rough idea. Get a real brief.

Type a half-formed thought. This turns it into a professional creative brief, on-brand and grounded in the Leak system, before a single word of content gets written. Bring the brief back to the Studio and it becomes production-ready content.

This is Steps 1 to 3 of the Studio workflow. The generator sets the goal, the concept and the direction. To finish a piece, bring this brief to the Studio and it runs the rest:
  1. Design every frame (layout, hierarchy, type, colour, photography, motion).
  2. Curate any photos you upload (the strongest one, crops, edits, sequencing).
  3. Write the copy in every format (caption, LinkedIn, email, blog).
  4. Audit against the Brand System, Creative OS, Playbook and Product facts. If it weakens Leak, reject and improve.

Built from the Leak system: the content pillars, real sourced proof (ATO benchmarks, the 2026 wage/super/surcharge changes), the Creative OS visual direction and the Founder Brain voice. It runs entirely in your browser. It is a smart starting brief, not a live writer; treat its first draft as a strong opening move to sharpen.
