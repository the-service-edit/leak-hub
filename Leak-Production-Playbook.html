Every one of the 60 posts, engineered to publish: objective, hook, format, Reel script, art direction, Google Business post, email angle, posting time, reasoning and the engagement to expect. Built for Leak's four channels — Instagram (feed + Reels), LinkedIn, Google Business and email. Static graphics live in your Studio; Reels and video are scripted here to shoot.

The strategy

Leak is a B2B brand that sells a profit audit to café owners. So social is not a menu board — it is demand generation. The job of the feed is to make an overworked owner feel understood, prove we know their numbers better than they do, and make booking the obvious next step. We sell nothing most days on purpose: value earns the follow and the save; the offer converts the people value warmed up.

Who we're talking to

  • Primary: independent café owner-operators, AU, doing 40 jobs at once, time-poor, margin-anxious, proud, allergic to being sold to.
  • Secondary: multi-site operators and venue managers who feel the same squeeze at scale.
  • Amplifiers: accountants, bookkeepers and hospitality suppliers who refer — LinkedIn's real job.

What each channel is for

  • Instagram (feed + Reels): reach and emotional connection. Reels are the growth engine; the feed is the proof and the saves.
  • LinkedIn: trust and referral. Peer-credibility register, no emojis, longer thinking. This is where accountants decide to send you clients.
  • Google Business Profile: intent capture. People searching 'café accountant' or 'food cost help' are near-buying. Short, keyword-aware, always a link.
  • Email: nurture and conversion. Where the founding-5 and the guarantee do their closing work, to a list that already knows you.

The funnel: Value (weeks lean here) → Proof → Offer. Never three sell posts in a row. Every sell post is earned by the value posts around it.
